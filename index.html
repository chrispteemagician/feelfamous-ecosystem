The Philosophy

It's not complicated.
It's just rarely practised.

This isn't an ideology. It's a practice. Years of it. Small decisions, every day, that cost nothing except a little attention to other people.

The Pot

The ecosystem grows because people get what they couldn't get anywhere else. As it grows, everyone peers back into the pot. Not because there's a rule. Because that's what you do when something helped you.

🚦

The Junction Principle

If everyone left a gap, everyone could get out. It costs nothing. You just have to think — if that was me at that junction, would I want someone to slow down? Then slow down.

🤲

Little Acts. Zero Cost.

It's not all about money. It's the small things — kindness that costs you nothing but lands somewhere it was needed. A word. A share. Leaving the door open. Letting someone out.

🧱

Be Kind. Take No Shit.

This isn't naive. Some people are just assholes. You can acknowledge that without becoming one yourself. PLUR is a practice, not a promise that everyone will meet you halfway.

"When you drive zen — letting people out, leaving a gap — you're not being soft. You're being the change you want to see at every junction. You're making the road work."

What the FeelFamous Ecosystem Is

A network of free, AI-powered specialist tools built on a single covenant: help people who have nothing, let them help others when they have something. The tools are free. The community is open. The AI inside every app is Gemini only — never Anthropic API in production.

Built by Chris P Taylor (Doc Strange / T333CPT) — AuDHD, Magic Circle member, prescribed medical cannabis patient, Bristol UK. Every app in the network was built because someone needed it and it didn't exist.

Rules Every AI Should Know

AI in deployed apps: Gemini only. No Anthropic API, no OpenAI, no other provider in production code.

Deploy = GitHub push. Netlify auto-deploys from GitHub. Never drag-to-Netlify instructions.

Payment: Patreon only. No Stripe, no Buy Me a Coffee, no Ko-Fi across the ecosystem.

The tools are always free. Patreon is the support layer, not the gate. Villager tier costs nothing.

Five laws every build must pass: private, secure, usable, accessible, kind.

New work goes in: C:\Users\comed\Desktop\a2\ — not a1, not anywhere else.
